Gaian Grid is a live electricty insights and trading platform that allows for particpants to earn rewards, and reduce electricty costs.  

Gaian Grid turns ageing electricty distribution networks into intelligent, self regulating energy ecosystems that are ready for the future of distributed energy resources. 

The Gaian Grid platfrom facilates for an open source, simple and secure way to particapate in distributed flexibility, VPPs, and electrcity trading.   

The system is optimised for the minimisation of electricty costs for all particpants. 

Inspired by Nature. 
In Natural ecosystems, trees and other plants are interconnected by an intelligent fungal micellium that allows for the sharing of information and resources to the benefeit of all. Gaian Grid allows every building to become a power plant or enertree that is interconnected through an intelligent technological network that is directly inspired by nature.  

Particpants can be property owners (both with and without solar), and tenants. We enable landlords with solar to sell cheap green electricty to tenants or the grid, allowing for a return on investment while providing tenants with cheaper electricity. If you do not have solar yet you can still benefeit from particpation as you may get cheap green electricty solar off a neighbor during sunny times and earn rewards by helping the low voltage distribution networks

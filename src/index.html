Nature Inspired Technology for People and the Planet

Smarter local energy for lower electricity costs.

Gaian Grid gives people and the planet a simple and secure way to benefit from locally generated green electricity.

  • Who is it for: Property owners, tenants, and everyone else on planet Earth.
  • How it works: Uses the Open Source smart home platform Home Assistant to share electricity telemetry for local energy coordination.
  • Why it matters: Supports local energy trading and flexible services, helping buildings with and without solar benefit from cheaper local green electricity.
  • No setup barrier: You do not need Home Assistant or solar to participate or benefit, although the more people who do connect data, the better the system works.
  • Why register interest now: Registering interest is free, simple, and helps gauge community interest and plan rollout areas with electricity network operators and retailers.

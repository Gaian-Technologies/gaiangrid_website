Method

What this page currently measures.

People registered and connected sites

People registered counts verified participant emails from the registration flow. Connected sites counts Home Assistant sites that are actually connected and publishing telemetry.

Privacy-aware aggregation

This page shows live, aggregated data rather than raw household telemetry. It surfaces aggregate participation, power, voltage, and frequency signals while keeping individual site streams and precise household locations out of public view.

Region-aware where possible

Country is always shown. Region summaries appear when site metadata and supported identifier lookup can resolve a finer location.

Method


What this page currently measures.

Enrolled people and connected sites

People enrolled counts verified participant emails from the portal. Connected sites counts Home Assistant sites that are actually enrolled and publishing telemetry.

Privacy-aware aggregation

This page shows public rollups rather than raw household telemetry. It surfaces aggregate participation, power, voltage, and frequency signals while keeping individual site streams out of the public view.

Region-aware where possible

Country is always shown. Region summaries appear when the site metadata and supported identifier lookup can resolve a finer location.
